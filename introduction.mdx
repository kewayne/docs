Shiipp Warehouse is a cloud-based, multi-tenant logistics platform designed specifically for freight forwarders and logistics companies. It centralises package tracking, courier management, billing, flight manifests, and real-time analytics into a single workspace — so your team can operate faster, with fewer errors, and at any scale.

## Who It's For

Shiipp Warehouse is built for every role in the modern logistics chain:

- **Freight Forwarders** — Manage inbound and outbound shipments, track packages across multiple couriers, and keep customers informed with live tracking links.
- **Warehouse Managers** — Receive, sort, and dispatch packages from a single dashboard with full visibility into inventory and courier handoffs.
- **Courier Partners** — Integrate directly via API or the courier portal to receive manifests, update delivery statuses, and access billing summaries.

## What Problems It Solves

Running a freight forwarding operation means juggling courier contracts, customer queries, invoicing, and compliance — often across disconnected spreadsheets and email threads. Shiipp Warehouse eliminates that chaos by giving your business:

- A **single source of truth** for every package, from receipt to final delivery.
- **Automated billing and invoicing** tied directly to package data, reducing manual errors.
- **Courier-level branding and configuration**, so each partner operates within your platform under their own profile.
- **Webhook-driven integrations** that push real-time events to your existing tools without polling.
- **Flight manifest generation** for air freight compliance, produced in seconds from live shipment data.
