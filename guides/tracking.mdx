Shiipp Warehouse includes a fully public tracking portal that your customers can access without creating an account or logging in. Every package you log immediately receives a live tracking page showing carrier information, milestone events, and timestamps. You share a single URL and your customers get end-to-end visibility from prealert through final delivery.

## Tracking URL

Each package's tracking page lives at a predictable path on your Shiipp Warehouse subdomain:

```
https://yourapp.com/track/{tracking_number}
```

Replace `{tracking_number}` with the carrier tracking number entered at intake. The URL is safe to embed directly in shipment confirmation emails, SMS notifications, or customer portals.

<Tip>
  Add the tracking URL template to your shipment notification email so every confirmation message automatically includes a clickable link to the live tracking page. Most email platforms support simple string substitution — insert the tracking number variable where `{tracking_number}` appears.
</Tip>

## Tracking Timeline

When a customer opens a tracking page they see a clean, chronological timeline. Each milestone displays the event name, a timestamp, and any location or carrier note attached to it.

<Accordion title="Standard milestone events">
  | Milestone | Triggered when |
  |-----------|---------------|
  | **Received** | Package is logged in Shiipp Warehouse |
  | **In Transit** | Status updated to in-transit by staff or webhook |
  | **Arrived at Facility** | Package scanned at destination facility |
  | **Out for Delivery** | Courier marks the package for last-mile delivery |
  | **Delivered** | Final delivery confirmed |
</Accordion>

The carrier name and logo appear at the top of the timeline so customers immediately know which courier is handling their shipment.

## Sharing a Tracking Link

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find the package in the Data Explorer">
    Open **Packages** in the sidebar and use the search bar or filters to locate the package you want to share.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Copy the tracking number">
    Click the tracking number in the table to copy it to your clipboard. The number is highlighted and a **Copy** confirmation appears briefly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build the tracking URL">
    Append the tracking number to your base tracking URL:

    ```
    https://yourapp.com/track/1Z999AA10123456784
    ```

    Paste this URL into your customer communication channel — email, chat, or SMS.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Prealerts

When a courier sends a prealert via webhook before the physical shipment arrives, Shiipp Warehouse creates the package record immediately. Customers can start tracking from the **Prealert** stage — they see a timeline event indicating the shipment has been announced and is en route to your facility.

This gives customers early visibility and reduces inbound "where is my package?" inquiries during peak periods.

<Note>
  Prealerted packages appear in the **Prealerts** tab of the Packages view until physical receipt is confirmed. At that point, the package moves to the main list and the timeline updates with a **Received** event.
</Note>

## Status Exceptions

If a carrier reports an exception — such as a customs hold, address issue, or damage — the tracking page displays an **Exception** banner above the timeline. The most recent exception message from the carrier is shown to the customer.

To add a manual status note to the tracking page:

1. Open the package in the Data Explorer.
2. Click **Add Status Note** in the package detail panel.
3. Type your note — for example, "Package held at customs. Expected clearance in 2–3 business days."
4. Click **Save**. The note appears on the customer-facing tracking page immediately.

Manual notes are clearly labeled as staff notes so customers understand they are warehouse-provided updates rather than carrier-scanned events.
