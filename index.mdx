# Welcome to Shiipp Warehouse

**Shiipp Warehouse** is the all-in-one operations platform built for freight forwarders and logistics companies. From the moment a package arrives at your facility to the second it reaches its destination, Shiipp Warehouse gives you complete visibility and control — managing your warehouse floor, courier partnerships, automated billing cycles, and real-time shipment tracking in one unified workspace.

<Note>
  Shiipp Warehouse is a multi-tenant platform, meaning each company operates in its own secure, isolated environment. Your data, couriers, and settings are always private to your organization.
</Note>

## How to Get Up and Running

Follow these four steps to go from a brand-new account to fully operational on Shiipp Warehouse.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Account and Log In">
    Sign up at [app.shiipp.com](https://app.shiipp.com) using your business email. Once you verify your address, log in to access your dedicated warehouse dashboard. Your organization's workspace is provisioned automatically — no manual setup required.

    <Tip>
      If your company already has a Shiipp Warehouse account, ask your administrator to invite you directly from **Settings → Team Members** instead of creating a new organization.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Your First Courier Partner">
    Navigate to **Couriers → Add Partner** and select your courier from the supported network or enter custom carrier details. Configure service types, rate cards, and pickup schedules so Shiipp Warehouse can route packages intelligently from day one.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Receiving Packages">
    Head to **Packages → Receive** to begin logging inbound shipments. Scan barcodes or enter tracking numbers manually, assign packages to the correct client accounts, and place them in their designated warehouse zones. Every package gets a full activity timeline automatically.

    <Tip>
      Print Shiipp Warehouse labels at intake so packages are traceable at every subsequent scan point throughout your facility.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Webhooks for Real-Time Sync">
    Go to **Settings → Webhooks** and register your endpoint URL to start receiving live event notifications. Subscribe to events like `package.received`, `package.dispatched`, and `invoice.created` to keep your external systems — ERPs, customer portals, or custom dashboards — automatically up to date.

    <Note>
      All webhook payloads are signed with your secret key. Always verify the `X-Shiipp-Signature` header before processing events in your system.
    </Note>
  </Step>
</Steps>
