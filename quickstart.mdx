This guide walks you through everything you need to go from a blank workspace to your first tracked package. By the end, you'll have a courier partner configured, a package on record, and a live tracking link ready to share with your customer.

<Tip>
  Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) the first time you log in. Shiipp Warehouse supports any TOTP-compatible authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy, 1Password). Once enabled, your account requires a 6-digit code at every login — protecting your tenant data and all packages within it.
</Tip>

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your account">
    Open your Shiipp Warehouse URL. You can sign in with either your **email address** or your **Operator ID** — a numeric identifier assigned by your administrator, useful for shared operator accounts not tied to a personal email.

    Enter your password. If 2FA is enabled on your account (recommended), you'll be prompted for a 6-digit code from your authenticator app immediately after:

    ```text
    Email or Operator ID:  you@yourcompany.com
    Password:              ••••••••••••
    2FA Code:              482 917
    ```

    After a successful login, you are routed to the section of the platform that matches your role — admins land on the full company dashboard, while warehouse staff are taken directly to the package scanning module.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete your company profile">
    Navigate to **Settings → Company Profile** and fill in your organisation's details:

    - **Company name** — displayed on invoices and the customer tracking portal.
    - **Logo** — upload a PNG or SVG (recommended: 200 × 60 px) for white-label branding.
    - **Default currency and timezone** — used across billing, reports, and package timestamps.
    - **Contact email** — shown to customers on tracking pages and invoice footers.

    Save your changes. These settings apply globally to your tenant workspace.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add your first courier partner">
    Go to **Couriers → New Courier** and complete the courier creation form:

    | Field | Description |
    |---|---|
    | **Display Name** | The courier's name as it appears on tracking pages and invoices (e.g., `FastShip Express`) |
    | **Branding** | Upload the courier's logo and set a hex colour for their profile |
    | **Master AWB Prefix** | The prefix used for Master AWB sequence generation (e.g., `810` for AmeriJet) |
    | **House AWB Prefix** | The prefix used for individual House AWB numbers |
    | **Contact details** | Email and phone number for operational queries |

    Click **Save Courier**. The courier is immediately added to your list, API credentials are provisioned automatically, and the courier is ready to receive packages.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Receive your first package">
    Go to **Packages → New Package** and enter the shipment details:

    - **Tracking number** — assigned by you or auto-generated using the courier's AWB prefix.
    - **Weight** — enter in either **LBS or KG**; the platform converts between the two automatically.
    - **Customer name and contact** — the recipient's details, shown on their tracking page.
    - **Courier** — select the partner you just created.
    - **Status** — set to `Received` to mark the package as in your warehouse.

    Click **Save Package**. The package is now live in your system with a full audit trail and an auto-generated public tracking link.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Share the tracking link with your customer">
    Every package gets a public tracking URL the moment it's created. Find the package in your dashboard, open its detail view, and copy the tracking link:

    ```text
    https://yourapp.com/track/{tracking_number}
    ```

    Share this link with your customer via email, SMS, or your customer portal. The page updates automatically whenever the package status changes — no login required for the recipient.

    You can also retrieve tracking data programmatically using the public API endpoint (no authentication required):

    ```bash
    curl https://yourapp.com/api/track/FSE-00001
    ```
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  This quickstart covers dashboard usage only. For API-based package creation, courier integration, and webhook configuration, see the [Authentication](/authentication) page to generate your first API key.
</Note>
