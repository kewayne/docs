The Invoice Ninja integration bridges your warehouse billing workflow with your accounting system. When you publish an invoice inside Shiipp Warehouse, the platform automatically pushes it to Invoice Ninja — complete with line items, package fees, surcharges, and client details — so your books stay current without any manual data entry.

## What This Integration Does

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Automatic Invoice Push" icon="file-invoice">
    Every invoice you publish in Shiipp Warehouse is instantly created in Invoice Ninja as a new invoice with matching totals and line items.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Client Matching" icon="user-check">
    Clients are matched by company name across both platforms. If no match exists, Invoice Ninja creates a new client record automatically.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Line Item Mapping" icon="list">
    Package fees, handling charges, and surcharges map directly to Invoice Ninja line items, preserving your fee structure in the accounting record.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Payment Status Sync" icon="circle-check">
    When a payment is recorded in Invoice Ninja, the **Paid** status syncs back to Shiipp Warehouse so your invoice list stays accurate.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Prerequisites

Before connecting the integration, make sure you have the following:

- An active **Invoice Ninja** account (self-hosted or cloud)
- An **API token** generated from your Invoice Ninja account settings (**Settings → API Tokens → New Token**)
- Administrator access in Shiipp Warehouse to reach **Settings → Integrations**

## Connecting Invoice Ninja

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Integration Settings">
    In Shiipp Warehouse, navigate to **Settings → Integrations → Invoice Ninja**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Invoice Ninja API URL">
    Type your Invoice Ninja base URL into the **API URL** field. For cloud accounts, this is typically `https://app.invoiceninja.com`. For self-hosted installs, use your own domain (e.g., `https://invoices.yourcompany.com`).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Paste Your API Token">
    Paste the API token you generated in Invoice Ninja into the **API Token** field.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test the Connection">
    Click **Test Connection**. Shiipp Warehouse will make a verification call to Invoice Ninja and confirm the credentials are valid. Resolve any errors before proceeding.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save**. The integration is now active and will process all subsequent published invoices automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## How Sync Works

When you click **Publish** on an invoice in Shiipp Warehouse, the platform immediately sends the invoice data to Invoice Ninja via the API. The sync includes:

- **Client details** — matched to an existing Invoice Ninja client by company name, or created as a new client if no match is found
- **Line items** — each package fee, handling charge, and surcharge becomes a separate line item in Invoice Ninja
- **Totals** — subtotals, tax amounts, and grand totals are preserved exactly as they appear in Shiipp Warehouse
- **Invoice number** — the Shiipp Warehouse invoice number is carried over so both systems reference the same document

<Note>
  Clients in Invoice Ninja are matched by **company name**. Ensure names are consistent across both systems — even small differences like `"Acme Corp"` vs `"Acme Corporation"` will cause a new client record to be created in Invoice Ninja instead of matching the existing one.
</Note>

## Manual Sync

If automatic sync fails (for example, due to a temporary network issue or an Invoice Ninja downtime), you can trigger a manual sync directly from the invoice detail page.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Invoice">
    Go to **Invoices** and click the invoice you want to sync.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Trigger Manual Sync">
    On the invoice detail page, click the **Sync to Invoice Ninja** button in the actions menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Success">
    A confirmation banner appears when the sync completes. If the sync fails again, check your API token and Invoice Ninja connectivity, then retry.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Use Invoice Ninja's built-in **payment portal** to collect payments from courier partners and clients. Once a payment is recorded there, Shiipp Warehouse will automatically reflect the **Paid** status on the corresponding invoice during the next sync — no manual status updates needed.
</Tip>
